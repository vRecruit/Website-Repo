About Us

VRecruit helps the world’s leading brands to hire digital and hire fair at scale.

Our Mission

Our mission is to create a website which will provide a platform for companies, institutes and schools to reliably conduct virtual interviews yielding accurate results which can be further used for hiring and other assessments.

Our Plan

vRecruit will offer services such as client specific interview process which uses Machine Learning based references to context provided by the client along with a system consisting of facial expression recognition and text based sentiment analysis.

Our Vision

The lack of any online platform providing services such as online interviews, viva, etc for institutes, companies and schools leads to loss of both time and resources which are spent on conducting these events. These can easily be automated with the introduction of Machine Learning and Natural Language Processing which will streamline the entire process.
